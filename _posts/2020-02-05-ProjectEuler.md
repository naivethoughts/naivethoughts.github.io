## Problem 25 : <a href="https://projecteuler.net/problem=25" target="_blank">
Fibonacci Series </a>
The Fibonacci series is, <br/>
$$ F(n) = F(n-1) + F(n-2) $$
$$ F(1) = F(1) + F(0) = 1 + 0 = 1 $$
